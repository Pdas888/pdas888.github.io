About

PhD student at UNSW Canberra focused on integral-field spectroscopy of Type Ia supernova remnants. I study ejecta morphology, shock diagnostics, and develop ML pipelines for spectral classification and 3D reconstruction (MUSE, HST, Python).

Research highlights

  • Calcium as a fingerprint of double detonation. Discovered a double-shell [Ca XV] structure in SNR 0509–67.5 using deep MUSE — first photographic evidence linking morphology to a sub-Chandrasekhar double-detonation (Nature Astronomy / press coverage).
  • Helium diagnostics in Type Ia remnants. First detection of broad & narrow He I and He II across three LMC remnants using archival MUSE; developed diagnostics to constrain pre-shock neutral He fractions (paper in progress).
  • Machine learning for 3D reconstruction. A supervised ML pipeline to detect and fit broad emission in tens of thousands of MUSE spectra enabling automated 3D kinematic mapping.
