PhD student | Supernova Remnants | UNSW Canberra

I am a PhD student in observational astrophysics, studying Type Ia supernova remnants using optical integral-field spectroscopy. My research focuses on the structure of the reverse-shocked ejecta, element stratification, and the connection between observed morphologies and explosion physics.

I develop machine learning frameworks to classify and reconstruct 3D spectral structures, and work on bridging observations with hydrodynamic models of white dwarf explosions. My goal is to reveal the physical conditions and origins of Type Ia supernovae.
